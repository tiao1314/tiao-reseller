RETURNS & REFUNDS

We want you to love what you buy. If something isn’t right, any item can be exchanged within 7 days of it reaching you. To make sure your return goes through smoothly, please check off every point below before sending anything back:

  • Keep it boxed. Send the item back in the packaging it arrived in whenever that packaging is part of the product itself — for instance, the shoe box that comes with trainers.
  • Tags stay on. If the piece came with tags attached, they need to still be attached and undamaged when it returns to us.
  • As good as new. The item can’t show any wear or signs of washing, and it must be free of damage — the only exception being a genuine fault.

Arrived faulty? Let us know and we’ll make it right. Ready to start a return? Find your order and get in touch with your reference to hand.
