RETURNS & REFUNDS

All items purchased can be exchanged only up to 7 days after receiving your order. To complete your return successfully, please make sure all of the following are met:

  • The item is returned in its original packaging where that packaging forms part of the goods — for example, the box for boxed items such as trainers.
  • Where an item has tags attached, please make sure they remain attached and undamaged on return.
  • The item shows no signs of wear or washing, and no damage — unless the item is faulty.

If your item arrived faulty, contact us and we’ll put it right. Need to start a return? Find your order and get in touch with your reference.
